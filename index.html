Nyanners

Nyatasha Nyanners, commonly known as Nyanners, is an independent female YouTuber, voice actress, and singer. She is well known for her soft voice and rap music covers. She joined VShojo in 2020 and left the company in April 2023. She is currently an employee of Mythic Talent.

Nyanners - the only girl worth crying for

Background

Nyanners first experimented with voice acting and singing through 4chan message boards, with bizarre and satirical takes on hentai cultured panels. The name Nyanners originated as a friendly diminutive of nyannyanoxo, an ironic anime fan name chosen when she joined a 4chan /a/ raid on the Chatango room for the Naruto Fanon Wiki, intended to mock Seireito Hyuga's exceptionally long entry and his long list of abilities.
